Project Work

RunMate
Previous Next

RUNMATE: This was my final individual project at Hackbright. RunMate is a web app that aims to find running partners based on your proximity and mile time. As soon as you log in, the app will find your location using HTML5 Geolocation and display runs that you can go on with people that are within 5 miles from you and that run at a similar mile time. If you see no runs that you want to go on, or if you want to make your own run, you can choose to "run now" or "schedule a run". Using Google Maps draggable markers, you can choose where you want to start your run. When someone chooses to run with you, the app will notify you and your running partner via text and the user's inbox, where you can either accept or reject the run by texting back to the app or by rejecting on your inbox.

TECHNOLOGY STACK: Flask, Python, HTML5 Geolocation, Twilio API, Google Maps API, Bootstrap, SQLite3, SQLAlchemy, Chart.js, HTML/CSS

Wanderlust
Previous Next

WANDERLUST: Completed in 24 hours at the Emirates Hackathon, Wanderlust is a web app that aims to simplify group traveling. Given two different locations and budgets, Wanderlust will find competitive airline prices for two friends wishing to travel to the same airport. The flights the app gives are within the budget specified, or else it will find the cheapest flight. The app also includes AirBnB information close to the destination airport for the 2 guests to find lodging, as well as uses the Xola API to find events close by.

TECHNOLOGY STACK: Google Flights API, Google Maps API, Xola API, AirBnb request calls, Python, Flask, Twitter Bootstrap, HTML/CSS, SQLite3, JavaScript.
